Contact Link's Label and Title

I acknowledged this issue already, but never have the time to fix it. So here is it! before the changes the label for email link is 'Inquire'. But I thought it's somewhat unusual word for some peoples. I replaced it with 'Contact' as it's more common to see.

I also changed the title into something more clear. Title is the text appear when you hover on a link. It's now written 'Mail me at daavidhc@gmail.com' instead of only 'Inquire'. According to your personal judgement, which one is much more easier to understand?

It's also fixed the issue where mailto link do nothing when clicked if no email client is installed on your local computer or no action is set when mailto link is clicked. It's an issue where I'm the owner cannot fix because it's completely your computer's problem. By showing my email as the link title ensure user still could email me with 3rd party application such as Gmail.

Blockquote's left padding

I used vw (viewport width) unit to determine blockquote left padding. But I noticed inconsistency in styling because vw is a relative unit. It's basically based on user's monitor resolution. Wider screen results in bigger number. Therefore, I changed it to px which is an absolute unit. It appears consistently among all screen size.
